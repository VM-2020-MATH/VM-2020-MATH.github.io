About Me

I am now an Assistant Professor at the Department of Faculty of Science (Applied Mathematics), Shahid Rajaee Teacher Training University.
I am working in Applied Mathematics, which involves solving partial differential equations (PDEs) via numerical methods (mesh-dependent and meshless approaches)
and analyzing their errors. Also, I specifically am studying the mathematical models in oncology. Other research interests include machine learning algorithms
for solving PDEs and approximation problems.

Research Interests

Numerical solution of Partial Differential Equations (PDEs), Meshless Approximations, Radial Basis Functions (RBFs) interpolation,
Moving Least Squares (MLS) approximation, Mathematical Biology, Phase-Field models, Machine Learning (ML) algorithms, Artificial Intelligence.
