About Me

I am now an assistant professor at the Department of Faculty of Science (Applied Mathematics), Shahid Rajaee Teacher Training University. I am working in Applied Mathematics, which involves solving partial differential equations (PDEs) via numerical methods (mesh-dependent and meshless approaches) and analyzing their errors. Also, I specifically am studying the mathematical models in oncology. Other research interests include machine learning algorithms for solving PDEs and approximation problems.
